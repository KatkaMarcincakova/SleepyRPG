Story
After a long day our hero finally lays down and drifts into sleep, entering the world of dreams, where he's awaited by a nightmare, which he attempts to evade. Along the way, he's met with many other nightmares that aim to possess his mind. He also meets a few dreams along his journey, that allow him to reminisce of simpler and nicer times, as well as allowing him to take a deeper look within himself. His main goal is to reach the edge of the world of dreams and beat his biggest nightmare once and for all, which is the manifestation of all his fears and anxieties. At the end, he finds himself awake, in the real world, realizing he's been in a coma for the past few years. Is it the real world? What's real?
This game is like a dream - It has both happy and scary moments. But the most important thing is to not give up, and win against the nightmares.
